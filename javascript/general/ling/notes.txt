Grammar: Set of structural rules governing the composition of clauses
, phases and words

Morphology: study of the formation of words and how they relate to others words in the language

Syntax: set of rules that governs the structure of sentences in a language

Semantics: study of meaning, focus on the relationship of signifiers, words
, phrases, signs and symbols.

Pragmatics: Study the ways that context contribute to meaning.

Lexicon: language inventory of lexemes(words).

Semiotics: the study of meaning-making sign processes and meaningfull 
communication.
